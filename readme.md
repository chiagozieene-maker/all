> A modern, secure, and user-friendly platform connecting Personal Support Workers with families and elderly individuals in need of care services.

## 🌟 Overview

CarePal Scheduler is a comprehensive healthcare care-booking application that matches verified caregivers with service seekers based on location, skills, and availability. Built with privacy, compliance, and user experience at its core.

## ✨ Key Features

### For Service Seekers
- 🔍 **Smart Search & Filter** - Find caregivers by location, service type, experience, and availability
- 📅 **Easy Scheduling** - Book one-time or recurring services with flexible timing
- ⭐ **Verified Reviews** - Read authentic reviews from real families
- 💬 **Secure Messaging** - Real-time chat with caregivers before and after service
- 💰 **Transparent Pricing** - No hidden fees, clear cost breakdown
- 📱 **Service Tracking** - Monitor upcoming and completed bookings
- 🔔 **Notifications** - Real-time alerts for confirmations and reminders

### For Caregivers
- 👥 **Flexible Work** - Accept/decline job requests on your terms
- 📈 **Earnings Dashboard** - Track income and payments
- 📍 **GPS Tracking** - Check-in/out with geo-location verification
- ⭐ **Build Reputation** - Collect reviews and ratings
- 📋 **Client Profiles** - Access medical history and special requirements
- 🔐 **Secure Identity** - Background verified badge

### Platform Features
- 🔐 **GDPR/HIPAA Compliant** - Enterprise-grade data protection
- 🛡️ **Background Verification** - All caregivers thoroughly vetted
- 💳 **Secure Payments** - PCI-DSS compliant payment processing
- 📊 **Analytics** - Detailed reporting for administrators
- 🌐 **Multi-language Support** - Accessible to diverse communities
- 📱 **Mobile Responsive** - Works seamlessly on all devices
